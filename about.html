MY JOURNEY


My career plan revolves around web development and software development, where I aim to innovate solutions that enhance user experiences and streamline processes.

My journey into computer science began during my freshman year of high school and unfolded over the course of four transformative years. Throughout the four years, I was slowly introduced into the world of computer science as I learned coding languages, algorithms, and software development. I found myself captivated by the potential of what could be created with lines of code. By senior year, I learned about the endless possibilities of computer science, from product design to artificial intelligence to cybersecurity.

I decided to major in Computer Science in university.

Transitioning from high school computer science classes to college was a significant leap that both challenged and enriched my learning journey. I stepped into a more advanced realm of coding, algorithms, and theory. The college computer science courses demanded a deeper level of understanding and critical thinking, pushing me to sharpen my problem-solving skills.. Learning to ask for help, whether from professors or peers, became an essential skill.

In addition to academic advancements, I also participated in events outside the classroom such as hackathons. These fast-paced events taught me rapid learning and effective teamwork. Collaborating with a diverse team, we created our shared vision into reality under tight deadlines. The experience showed me the power of efficient problem-solving and the excitement of transforming ideas into tangible projects. Hackathons, with their intense creativity and collaborative energy, left a lasting impression on me. Because of this, I am enthusiastic about shaping technology’s future by combining creativity and problem-solving in meaningful ways.
